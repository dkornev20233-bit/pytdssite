What is pyTDS?

pyTDS is a Tower Defense game built entirely in Python using Pygame. Place defensive towers on a 1920×1080 map, hold the line against escalating waves of enemies, earn coins from kills, and upgrade your defenses. Survive all waves — or challenge yourself with Fallen, Frosty and Hardcore modes that push to 40-50 waves with brutal new enemy types.

🐍
Pure Python
No engine — built from scratch with Python and Pygame. Fully open-source, lightweight, runs at 60 FPS.
🏰
5 Game Modes
Easy (20 waves), Fallen (40 waves), Frosty (40 ice waves), Hardcore (50 waves), and Sandbox. Each has a unique enemy roster and final boss.
🌐
Multiplayer Co-op
Play Fallen mode co-op over LAN or via ngrok. Both players build towers together against enemies with +50% HP.
// gameplay

How it works

01
Enemies spawn in waves
Each wave spawns a mix of enemy types — Normal, Fast, Slow, Hidden, Armored, Breaker and more. They follow a fixed path and get tougher every round.
02
Place & upgrade towers
Choose from 24 tower types — Assassin, Accelerator, Archer, Militant, Cowboy, Swarmer, Harvester and more. Each has up to 6 upgrade levels.
03
Survive the final boss
Easy ends with Grave Digger (5,000 HP). Fallen builds to the Fallen King (175,000 HP). Frosty culminates in the Frost Spirit (500,000 HP).
