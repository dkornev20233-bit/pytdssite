📋 Speedrun Rules
General Rules
  • All runs must be recorded with video proof — no exceptions.
  • Video must clearly show the full run from start to finish, including the timer.
  • Runs without a visible in-game or external timer will be rejected.
  • Splicing, editing, or cutting the footage is strictly prohibited.
Video Requirements
  • Submit a YouTube, Twitch clip, or direct video link.
  • Video must be publicly accessible at the time of submission.
  • Minimum resolution: 720p. Audio is not required but preferred.
  • The game window must be clearly visible and unobstructed.
Timing
  • Any% — Time starts on game launch, ends when the final wave is cleared.
  • 100% — All towers must be placed and all waves completed.
  • Wave 30 — Time ends on the first frame wave 30 is completed.
  • No Hit — Complete the game without losing a single HP. Any damage to your base disqualifies the run. Video must clearly show the HP counter at all times.
  • No Upgrades — No tower upgrades allowed. Verified by video.
Verification
  • All submitted runs go through admin review before appearing on the leaderboard.
  • Suspicious runs may be flagged for extended review or rejected.
  • Moderators reserve the right to reject any run that violates the spirit of the rules.
  • Admins reserve the right to publish incomplete records.
