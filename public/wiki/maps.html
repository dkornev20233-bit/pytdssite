Maps

Three map layouts — each changes how enemies move and where you can place towers

Straight

The default map. Enemies enter from the left edge and march horizontally to the right edge along a single path at Y=432 (center-ish). Available in Easy, Fallen, and Sandbox.

Tip: The straight path gives maximum tower coverage — towers placed near the middle of the screen cover the most path distance.

Zigzag

Enemies follow a multi-segment path that snakes across the full screen. Waypoints:

SegmentDirectionFromTo
1→ RightLeft edge (−45, 290)(400, 290)
2↓ Down(400, 290)(400, 846)
3→ Right(400, 846)(766, 846)
4↑ Up(766, 846)(766, 106)
5→ Right(766, 106)(1167, 106)
6↓ Down(1167, 106)(1167, 846)
7→ Exit(1167, 846)Right edge

Frosty (4-lane)

Exclusive to Frosty mode. Four lanes converge on a central point at approximately (960, 480). Each lane approaches from a different screen edge:

  • Lane 0 — Top: enters top-center, moves straight down
  • Lane 1 — Bottom: enters bottom-center, moves straight up
  • Lane 2 — Left: enters left side, moves right
  • Lane 3 — Right: enters right side, moves left
Note: All four lanes converge at the same center point. Towers placed at the center cover all 4 lanes simultaneously.
