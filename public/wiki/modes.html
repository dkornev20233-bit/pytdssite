Fallen King — Boss Mechanics

The Fallen King appears at wave 40 of Fallen mode. It has several active abilities:

  • Charge — randomly speeds up to 55 px/s and turns hidden for ~1 second every 7–15s.
  • Sword Lunge — leaps toward a tower, pauses, then returns to path. Cooldown 9–15s.
  • Hero Summon — summons Fallen Hero enemies periodically (every 7–15s).
  • 5% armor — slight damage reduction. Slow resistance 50%.

True Fallen King — Additional Abilities (deleted, only sandbox)

Warning: True Fallen King is no longer encountered in normal play. It can only be spawned manually in Sandbox mode.
  • Phase Shift — becomes fully immune to damage for 2 seconds at <50% HP. Cooldown 25s.
  • Curse Wave — AoE expanding ring that stuns towers. Cooldown 12–20s.
  • Blood Charge — fast dash (90 px/s), turns hidden, leaves fire trail. Cooldown 8–14s.
  • Dark Summon — summons Fallen Shield + Fallen Hero pairs. Cooldown 10–18s.
  • 10% armor, 80% slow resistance

Towers vs Zombies (Event) (Sandbox Exclusive)

The "Towers vs Zombies" event (or TvZ for short, inspired by the Plants vs. Zombies genre) is a unique mode accessible exclusively through the admin panel in Sandbox mode.

1. Board Structure

Unlike standard maps with winding paths, this mode completely changes the level geometry:

  • 5×9 Grid: The playing field is divided into 5 horizontal rows and 9 columns.
  • Straight Paths: In each of the 5 rows, enemies move in a strictly horizontal line from right to left.
  • Placement Zones: Units (towers) are placed in the cells of this grid, defending their lanes.

2. Economy and Waves

The mode features unique progression settings:

  • Starting Budget: The player is given a fixed amount of $500.
  • No Kill Rewards: In this mode, no money is awarded for killing zombies (lmoney and bmoney are 0), forcing the player to strategically use their initial capital.
  • Wave Count: The event consists of 10 waves of increasing difficulty.

3. Wave Composition (WAVE_DATA)

Example of enemy progression in the event:

  • Waves 1–3: Only normal zombies (1 to 3 units).
  • Wave 5: A group of BucketZombies (2), normal zombies (3), and Coneheads (2).
  • Wave 8: A reinforced squad: Bucket (5), Normal (5), Conehead (5), and Football (3).
  • Wave 10: The final challenge featuring the most powerful units.

How to launch:

Since the event is hidden, it can only be activated in Sandbox mode via the Admin Panel or special debug commands that switch CURRENT_MAP to the TvZ grid and load TVZ_WAVE_DATA.
