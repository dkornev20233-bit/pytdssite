Fallen King — Boss Mechanics

The Fallen King appears at wave 40 of Fallen mode. It has several active abilities:

  • Charge — randomly speeds up to 55 px/s and turns hidden for ~1 second every 7–15s.
  • Sword Lunge — leaps toward a tower, pauses, then returns to path. Cooldown 9–15s.
  • Hero Summon — summons Fallen Hero enemies periodically (every 7–15s).
  • 5% armor — slight damage reduction. Slow resistance 50%.

True Fallen King — Additional Abilities (deleted, only sandbox)

Warning: True Fallen King is no longer encountered in normal play. It can only be spawned manually in Sandbox mode.
  • Phase Shift — becomes fully immune to damage for 2 seconds at <50% HP. Cooldown 25s.
  • Curse Wave — AoE expanding ring that stuns towers. Cooldown 12–20s.
  • Blood Charge — fast dash (90 px/s), turns hidden, leaves fire trail. Cooldown 8–14s.
  • Dark Summon — summons Fallen Shield + Fallen Hero pairs. Cooldown 10–18s.
  • 10% armor, 80% slow resistance
