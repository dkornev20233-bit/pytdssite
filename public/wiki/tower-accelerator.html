Mechanics

Fires a continuous purple laser beam at the furthest enemy in range.

  • At Lv1 gains Hidden Detection.
  • At Lv4–5 fires a dual laser — hits 2 targets simultaneously.
  • Range was recently increased on Lv3-5 for better late-game performance.
  • Very high DPS — one of the top single-target damage towers in the game.

Tips

  • Best in mid-to-late game due to high placement cost.
  • Lv4–5 dual laser significantly increases total damage output.
  • Max 5 on field — even 2–3 Accelerators cover most situations.
  • Does not pierce — use Frost Blaster or Archer for multi-enemy coverage.
