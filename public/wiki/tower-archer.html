Arrow Modes

The Archer has several arrow modes selectable from the tower panel (Ice Arrow removed):

  • Arrow (default) — standard piercing arrow.
  • Fire Arrow (unlocks Lv1) — deals burn damage over time.
  • Stun Arrow (unlocks Lv2) — briefly stuns enemies on hit.
  • Explosion Arrow (unlocks Lv4) — arrows explode on contact for AoE damage.
  • Poison Arrow (unlocks Lv5) — applies poison damage over time.

Mechanics

  • Placement Restriction: Archer can no longer be placed directly on roads.
  • Bouncing Arrows: Piercing arrows now bounce backwards after hitting their last target, potentially hitting enemies again.
  • Fires a homing arrow that tracks the locked target.
  • Hidden Detection unlocks at Lv4.

Tips

  • Place Archer slightly away from the path to utilize its increased range and satisfy placement rules.
  • The bouncing mechanic is extremely powerful on straight paths.
  • Cheat sheet: Fire for DoT, Stun for fast enemies, Explosion for groups.
