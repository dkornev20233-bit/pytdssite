Arrow Modes

The Archer has three arrow modes selectable from the tower panel:

  • Arrow (default) — standard piercing arrow, always available.
  • Ice Arrow (unlocks Lv2) — slows enemy to 55% speed for 0.6 seconds on hit.
  • Flame Arrow (unlocks Lv3) — applies fire DoT: 2 damage every 0.5s for 3 seconds.
Mode switching is done via the buttons in the tower info panel. Arrows home toward the target.

Mechanics

  • Fires a homing arrow that tracks the locked target.
  • After hitting the primary target the arrow continues straight (no re-target).
  • Pierce count determines how many enemies one arrow passes through.
  • Hidden Detection unlocks at Lv4.

Tips

  • Ice Arrow is excellent for slowing fast enemies (Scout, Fallen Rusher).
  • Flame Arrow deals good DoT against tanky enemies (Fallen Giant, bosses).
  • High pierce at Lv5 (6 pierce) makes it great in Straight map where enemies line up.
  • Cheap at 400 — good early tower for economy builds.
