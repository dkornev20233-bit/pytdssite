Mechanics

Single-target tower that attacks the furthest enemy in range.

  • At Lv1 gains Hidden Detection — can target invisible enemies.
  • At Lv2 unlocks Whirlwind Slash — an AoE ability that hits all enemies in range. Cooldown: 10 seconds.
  • Whirlwind base damage: 15 dmg. Lv3 adds +15, Lv4 adds +10 more (total 40 at max).
  • Whirlwind correctly targets hidden enemies if hidden detection is active.

Tips

  • Good early-game tower due to low cost (300 coins).
  • Upgrade to Lv2 ASAP for Whirlwind AoE against grouped enemies.
  • Max 5 on field — stack several for high single-target + AoE coverage.
  • Pairs well with Sledger or Freezer to slow enemies into Whirlwind range.
