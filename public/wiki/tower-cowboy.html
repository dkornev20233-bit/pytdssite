There ain't enough room for the two of us pardner... Gain cash on reload. A hybrid tower that provides both offense and economy.

Mechanics

  • Cash on Reload: Earns money every time it reloads its weapons. Income was recently increased across all levels.
  • Reload Rework: Reload times were significantly buffed (e.g., Lv0 reload 2.5s -> 1.5s).
  • Visual Projectiles: Now fires actual visual projectiles instead of hitscan.
  • Max Limit: Up to 14 Cowboys can be placed on the field.

Tips

  • Place early to start generating income immediately.
  • Cowboy's reload speed is key to its income; upgrading to Lv2 provides a huge boost to cash flow.
  • Excellent for aggressive early-game defense and snowballing your economy.
