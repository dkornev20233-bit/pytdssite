Mechanics

Generates passive income every wave. Has no attack, no range, and cannot damage enemies.

  • Income is added at the start of each wave.
  • Stacks directly with multiple Farms — 8 Lv5 Farms = +12,800 coins per wave.
  • Cheapest tower in the game at 250 coins.
  • No hidden detection, no upgrades to combat stats.
Total investment for 1 Farm maxed (Lv5): 250 + 200 + 600 + 1200 + 2500 + 4500 = 9,250 coins. At Lv5 it pays back in ~6 waves.

Tips

  • Place Farms early (waves 1–5) so they generate income through the whole game.
  • Sell Farms in late game to fund tower upgrades — they have a sell value.
  • 8× Lv5 Farms = +12,800 coins/wave — enough to buy an Accelerator every wave.
  • In Fallen mode, Farms are essential due to the high cost of Fallen-tier upgrades.
