Mechanics

Fires homing ice bullets that slow enemies on hit.

  • If a stronger slow hits an already-slowed enemy → the stronger slow overrides.
  • The slow timer is always refreshed on a new hit from the same Freezer.
  • Multiple Freezers stack their slows — the highest active slow wins.
  • Does not freeze enemies — only slows. Use Frostcelerator or Sledger for full freeze.
  • No hidden detection at any level.

Tips

  • Cheap (400) and stackable (max 6) — good value for economy builds.
  • Best used against fast enemies to prevent them from escaping the range of other towers.
  • Combine with Assassin or Gladiator to keep enemies in AoE range longer.
  • At Lv4 the 15% slow with 5s duration is very reliable.
