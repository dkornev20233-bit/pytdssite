Mechanics

Fires a piercing ice bullet that travels through every enemy in its path (unlimited pierce).

  • Applies slow (50–75%) to each enemy it passes through.
  • Freeze stacking: each hit from Frost Blaster adds 1 freeze charge. After 3 hits, the enemy is frozen for 0.45–1.5s (the counter resets).
  • Armor Shred (Lv2+): permanently reduces the enemy's armor value by 15% (applied once per enemy per battle, not per hit).
  • Defense Drop (Lv4): each hit deals +35% bonus damage multiplier.
  • Hidden Detection unlocks at Lv1.
  • Bullet travels up to 1100 pixels before disappearing.
Armor Shred at Lv2 is very powerful against armored Fallen enemies — place Frost Blasters early against Fallen Armor, Possessed Armor, etc.

Tips

  • With max 14 on field, Frost Blaster is the most scalable tower in the game.
  • At Lv4 the 53 damage + 35% def drop = very high effective DPS.
  • The piercing bullet rewards map positions where it crosses many enemies at once (Straight path mid-screen).
  • Combine with Sledger for freeze → double damage synergy.
