Mechanics

Fires an ice laser beam that hits up to 4 enemies simultaneously. Always has hidden detection.

  • Slow: reduces enemy speed by 25% (multiplied by enemy's slow resistance). Active while in range.
  • Freeze mechanic: after dealing 1000 cumulative damage (to non-frozen enemies), all current targets are frozen for 2.5 seconds. Counter resets per freeze cycle.
  • Speed is restored when the enemy leaves range or the Frostcelerator is sold.
  • Max 1 on field — can only place one Frostcelerator per game.
Freeze does NOT work on enemies with slow immunity (Frost Acolyte, Frost Spirit).

Tips

  • Place in the middle of a long path for maximum uptime against the same enemies.
  • The freeze is most valuable on fast enemies — frozen Scouts and Fallen Rushers become easy targets.
  • Works best combined with Accelerator, Sledger or Assassin since it does moderate damage on its own.
  • 1000 cumulative damage threshold = roughly 50–200 hits depending on upgrade level.
