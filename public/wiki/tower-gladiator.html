Mechanics

Swings a sword in a 180° arc hitting all enemies in the half-circle in front of it.

  • The swing arc is centered on the current target — positioning the tower optimally matters.
  • Hidden Detection is now available from Lv0.
  • Max 6 on field — placement was recently buffed from 2 to 6.
  • Has a Stun Block mechanic — can block one stun effect from an enemy ability, then goes on cooldown.
  • Swing arc trail is visually shown for 0.18 seconds after each swing.
Max 6 on field — place them at choke points where the arc covers the most enemies.

Tips

  • Excellent on Zigzag map where enemies turn corners — the 180° arc can hit enemies coming from two directions.
  • Lv5 is a massive DPS jump (78 dmg, range 6.5) — but very expensive.
  • Gladiator has been heavily rebalanced: damage was nerfed at early levels but placement limit was increased.
