Hallow Punk

🧨
Hallow Punk
Cost 3,500 💰
Max on field 4
Upgrade levels 6

Description

A mad explosives expert capable of knocking back and burning enemies. A powerful AoE and crowd control tower with a spooky theme.

Mechanics

  • Explosive AoE: Attacks hit all enemies in a radius around the target.
  • Knockback: Explosions can push enemies back.
  • Burn Effect: High level upgrades apply a lasting burn effect to enemies.

Tips

  • Place where enemies tend to cluster to maximize AoE value.
  • Knockback helps in delaying faster enemies while your stronger towers finish them off.
  • Pairs extremely well with slowing towers to create a deadly chokepoint.
