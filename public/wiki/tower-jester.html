Jester

🃏
Jester
Cost FREE 💰
Max on field 6
Upgrade levels 6

Description

I have a ton of tricks in my pockets! Cycle through and throw debuff bombs at enemies! A versatile support tower with various crowd control options.

Mechanics

  • Bomb Cycling: Cycles through different bomb types with unique effects.
  • Confusion Bomb: Makes enemies walk backwards for a short duration. Cooldown: 6 seconds.
  • Poison Bomb: Deals damage over time.
  • Fire Bomb: High damage and burn effect.

Tips

  • Confusion is extremely powerful for buying time — place Jesters where enemies are most likely to leak.
  • If you have multiple identical bombs, they won't stack their effects simultaneously; use different bomb types for maximum impact.
  • Range was recently nerfed to 8.5 at max level, so place them closer to the path.
