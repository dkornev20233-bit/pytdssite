Mechanics

Fires homing red orbs at enemies. Its main mechanic is the coin bonus on kill:

  • Tracks total damage dealt to each enemy.
  • If this Lifestealer dealt ≥25% of the enemy's max HP and the enemy dies → earns bonus coins equal to X% of that enemy's max HP.
  • Example at Lv5 (45%): enemy with 3000 max HP → earns +1350 coins on kill.
  • Does not have hidden detection at any level.
The coin bonus is separate from the normal kill reward. Both are earned if conditions are met.

Tips

  • Extremely strong against high-HP enemies (Fallen Giant gives $5000 + 45% of 3000 = $6350 total at Lv5).
  • Place near high-HP targets to ensure it deals enough damage to trigger the bonus.
  • Combine with other damage towers to ensure kills — Lifestealer gets credit for its share.
  • The 400 coin placement cost makes it accessible early.
