Description

Trained with an assault rifle, this tower deals medium damage at a fast firerate. A solid starter tower for many strategies.

Mechanics

  • Starter Category: Easy to use and effective in the early waves.
  • Damage Adjustment: Damage was recently nerfed across several levels to balance its high firerate.
  • Fast Firerate: Excels at clearing groups of weak enemies.
  • Hidden Detection: Unlocks at level 2.

Tips

  • Militant is a reliable all-rounder, though its damage output is now more balanced.
  • Place near the start of the path to weaken enemies for your stronger towers.
  • Unlock cost: 300 coins in the shop.
