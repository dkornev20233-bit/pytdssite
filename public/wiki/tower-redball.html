Mechanics

The Red Ball physically leaps at its target, deals damage on contact, then returns to its home position.

  • While jumping the ball tracks the live position of the target — it won't miss if the enemy keeps moving.
  • Deals damage only on physical contact (melee on arrival).
  • Hidden Detection unlocks at Lv2.
  • Range is always 7 tiles, measured from the home position (not current ball position).
  • Jump speed: 600 px/s in both directions.
The firerate is the cooldown AFTER returning home — not the total cycle time. Actual DPS depends on distance to target.

Tips

  • High damage at Lv4 (100 per hit) — one of the hardest-hitting single-target towers.
  • Performs best against slow/medium-speed enemies due to travel time.
  • Red Ball was recently rebalanced with lower damage but more consistent performance at mid-levels.
  • The ball is invulnerable during flight — it always returns even if the target dies.
