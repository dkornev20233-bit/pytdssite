Mechanics

Melee tower with a crit rhythm — every 3rd attack is a critical hit.

  • Every 3rd swing deals damage × crit multiplier (1.75× to 4.0×).
  • Range was recently nerfed significantly on all levels — place closer to the path.
  • Hidden Detection at Lv1.
  • Bleed stacks (Lv2+): each swing adds N bleed stacks to the target. Each stack deals 0.5% of enemy max HP per tick.
  • Max 30 bleed stacks per enemy. Stacks accumulate across multiple swings.
  • Single target — does NOT pierce or AoE.
Bleed is extremely powerful against high-max-HP enemies — 30 stacks × 0.5% = 15% of max HP as bonus damage per tick cycle.

Tips

  • Strongest against tanks and bosses (Fallen Giant, Fallen King) due to bleed scaling with max HP.
  • Hidden Detection at Lv1 makes it useful against Hidden Bosses early.
  • Lv4 crit (×4.0) + 3 bleed stacks per hit = extremely high burst on the 3rd swing.
  • Max 3 on field — stack all 3 for maximum bleed application speed.
