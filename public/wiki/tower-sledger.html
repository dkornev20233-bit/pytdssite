Mechanics

Swings a hammer in an AoE arc hitting up to N enemies per swing.

  • Each hit adds a slow stack — slow accumulates per enemy up to the Max Slow cap.
  • Once slow reaches Max Slow → enemy is frozen for Freeze Dur (Lv3+).
  • At Lv4+ deals double damage to already-frozen or chilled enemies.
  • At Lv5 each swing has a 20% chance to launch an aftershock projectile dealing 20% bonus damage.
  • At Lv5 each swing reduces target armor by up to 10% (capped at 33% total).
  • Does NOT gain Hidden Detection at any level.
Max Slow cap means once an enemy is frozen and thaws, its slow resets — you can re-freeze by hitting again.

Tips

  • Strongest AoE tower for slowing — pairs exceptionally well with Frost Blaster (which deals 2× to frozen).
  • Place centrally on the path to maximize hits per swing.
  • Lv3 is the freeze unlock — a big power spike worth prioritizing.
  • Lv5 aftershock is rare (20%) but the armor drop per swing is consistent and strong.
