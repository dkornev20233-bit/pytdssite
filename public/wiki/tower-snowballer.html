Snowballer

❄️
Snowballer
Cost 1,200 💰
Max on field 8
Upgrade levels 6

Description

Attacks enemies with homing snowballs that slow them down. A classic defensive tower with reliable crowd control.

Mechanics

  • Homing Snowballs: Snowballs track their target, ensuring a hit.
  • Slow Effect: Each hit slows enemies for a few seconds. Slow percentage increases with level.
  • Visual Levels: Features visual level indicators in the upgrade menu.

Tips

  • Place near the start of your defense to slow down fast enemies early.
  • Highly effective when paired with high-damage, slow-firing towers like Accelerator.
  • Great for maintaining control over high-density waves.
