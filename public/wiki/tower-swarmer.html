Description

OH NOES, THE BEES! Attack enemies with bees that do damage over time. A powerful crowd-control and DoT tower.

Mechanics

  • Damage over Time: Bees attach to enemies and deal damage continuously. Damage was recently buffed (Lv0 dmg 5 -> 6).
  • Reload Buff: Reload time was reduced from 2.5s to 2.0s at level 0.
  • Stacking: Multiple bees can stack on a single enemy for increased damage.
  • Max Limit: Up to 14 Swarmers can be placed on the field.

Tips

  • Highly effective against slow, high-HP enemies like Bosses where DoT can fully tick.
  • Swarmer was recently buffed, making it a much more viable option for mid-game defense.
  • Pairs well with towers that slow enemies, giving the bees more time to work.
