Mechanics

Fires bursts of toxic pellets that apply stacking slow + poison DoT on hit.

  • Burst mechanic: fires N shots rapidly (0.108s between shots), then waits a cooldown before next burst.
  • At Lv4 there is no cooldown (0.0s) — it fires continuously in a 20-shot burst.
  • Slow stacks: each shot adds slow%, accumulating up to a max of 30% total.
  • Poison DoT: refreshes on each hit. Multiple Toxic Gunners can each maintain their own poison stack.
  • Armor reduction (Lv3+): each poison tick permanently reduces enemy armor by 3%, up to 50% total.
  • Hidden Detection at Lv3+.
The armor reduction from Lv3+ stacks over time — a long fight against a tanky enemy will strip its armor completely.

Tips

  • Very effective against bosses and high-HP enemies due to DoT + armor stripping.
  • Lv4 is a massive power spike — 20-shot burst with no cooldown and 8 dmg/tick poison.
  • Max 3 on field limits total poison coverage but 3× stacked is very strong.
  • Combine with Sledger to slow-stack enemies while Toxic Gunner strips armor.
