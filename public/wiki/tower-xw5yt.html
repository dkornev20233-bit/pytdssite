Unique Abilities

xw5yt has 3 exclusive abilities that no other tower has:

  • pokaxw5yt (unlocks Lv1, cooldown 40s) — unique ability exclusive to this tower.
  • hixw5yt (unlocks Lv2, cooldown 25s) — the tower temporarily freezes itself (stops attacking) for a special effect.
  • chiter (unlocks Lv4, cooldown 35s) — boosts all friendly towers in range to 0.050s firerate for 8 seconds. This is the most powerful support ability in the game.
The chiter ability at Lv4 is extremely powerful — it temporarily turns every tower in range into a near-instant attacker for 8 seconds. Accelerators, Slashers, and Gladiators all benefit enormously.

Mechanics

  • Functions identically to Accelerator in base stats — green laser beam, same DMG/firerate progression.
  • At Lv4–5 fires a dual green laser targeting 2 enemies simultaneously.
  • Hidden Detection at Lv1.
  • Max 1 on field at any time.
  • Only available in Sandbox mode — does not appear in any game mode inventory or loadout.

Lore

xw5yt is a developer/special tower — its name references the game's creator. It is intentionally overpowered and hidden from normal gameplay.
